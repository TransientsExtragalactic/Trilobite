radiation.synchrotron.SEDs.one_zone_closure.invert_powerlaw_ssa_sed_demarchi#

radiation.synchrotron.SEDs.one_zone_closure.invert_powerlaw_ssa_sed_demarchi(nu_brk: float | ndarray | Quantity, F_nu_brk: float | ndarray | Quantity, *, p: float | ndarray = 3.0, f: float | ndarray = 0.5, theta: float | ndarray = 1.5707963267948966, epsilon_B: float | ndarray = 0.1, epsilon_E: float | ndarray = 0.1, gamma_min: float | ndarray = 1.0, gamma_max: float | ndarray = 1000000.0, redshift: float = None, luminosity_distance: _UnitBearingScalarLike = None, angular_diameter_distance: _UnitBearingScalarLike = None, proper_distance: _UnitBearingScalarLike = None, cosmology: cosmo.Cosmology = None) → tuple[ndarray, ndarray][source]#

Invert an SSA turnover using the DeMarchi et al. (2022) analytic formulae.

This is the public, unit-aware wrapper for the DeMarchi et al.[1] SSA inversion. It converts an observed turnover frequency and flux density to the inferred magnetic field strength \(B\) and emitting radius \(R\) using the DM22 broken-power-law closure.

Parameters:

  • nu_brk (float, array-like, or Quantity) – SSA turnover frequency \(\nu_{\rm brk}\). If unitless, it is interpreted in GHz.

  • F_nu_brk (float, array-like, or Quantity) – Flux density at the turnover. If unitless, it is interpreted in Jy.

  • p (float or array-like, optional) – Electron power-law index.

  • f (float or array-like, optional) – Effective filling factor of the emitting region.

  • theta (float or array-like, optional) – Pitch angle in radians.

  • epsilon_B (float or array-like, optional) – Fraction of internal energy in magnetic fields.

  • epsilon_E (float or array-like, optional) – Fraction of internal energy in relativistic electrons.

  • gamma_min (float or array-like, optional) – Minimum electron Lorentz factor.

  • gamma_max (float or array-like, optional) – Maximum electron Lorentz factor.

  • redshift (float, optional) – Source redshift used to resolve cosmological distances.

  • luminosity_distance (float, array-like, or Quantity, optional) – Luminosity distance to the source.

  • angular_diameter_distance (float, array-like, or Quantity, optional) – Angular-diameter distance to the source.

  • proper_distance (float, array-like, or Quantity, optional) – Proper distance to the source.

  • cosmology (Cosmology, optional) – Cosmology used to resolve missing distances.

Returns:

Two-element tuple (B, R) where

  • B is returned as a Quantity in G

  • R is returned as a Quantity in cm

Return type:

tuple

Notes

This closure is separate from the main Triceratops single-zone inversion family and specifically follows the DeMarchi et al. analytic treatment.

Applicability

Use this function when you want the DM22 SSA inversion based on an observed turnover modeled as a broken power law.

Important caveat

The implementation does not support \(p = 2\) and should not be used in that singular case without a separate treatment.
