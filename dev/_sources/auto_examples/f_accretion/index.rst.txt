**********************************************
Physics Backend Examples — Accretion Disks
**********************************************

This gallery showcases the :mod:`~triceratops.dynamics.accretion.one_zone` module:
time-dependent, one-zone accretion disk models driven by a compiled Cython
explicit-Euler integrator.

The examples progress from a minimal quickstart through physical model
comparisons to scenarios with external mass supply.  For theoretical background,
see :ref:`one_zone_disk_theory`.  For the full API, see :ref:`one_zone_disk`.
