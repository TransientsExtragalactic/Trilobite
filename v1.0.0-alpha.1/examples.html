Example Gallery#

Practical examples to get started with Trilobite.

Explore runnable, self-contained examples — from loading observational data and running inference to modeling synchrotron emission and accretion disk dynamics.

Working with Data#

Load and inspect your observations before fitting anything.

Observational Data

Load, group, and visualize photometric observations.

Load radio photometry from FITS files, group observations into temporal epochs, and plot light curves using the data subpackage.

Working With Radio Photometry
Optical Filters and Photometry

Build and convolve optical filters for broadband photometry.

Construct filters, assemble a FilterBundle for MCMC-optimised matrix-multiply convolution, and convert between flux density and AB/ST magnitudes.

Optical Filters and Photometry

Understanding the Physics#

Learn the radiative and dynamical building blocks that every Trilobite model is built from.

Synchrotron Emission

Understand the synchrotron emission that drives radio transients.

Four sub-sections: kernel functions and the \(\nu\)–\(\gamma\)–\(B\) relation; SED spectral regimes; electron cooling (synchrotron and IC); closure relations and equipartition analysis.

Synchrotron Emission Examples
Free-Free Emission

Compute free-free emission and absorption in ionized gas.

Thermal bremsstrahlung emissivity, flux density, optical depth profiles for wind and shell CSM geometries, and FFA attenuation of synchrotron SEDs using free_free.

Free-Free Emission Examples
Opacity Laws

Evaluate grey and frequency-dependent opacity laws.

Kramers, electron scattering, and OPAL/TOPS Rosseland mean opacity from the bundled tables using opacity.

Opacity Models
Shock Dynamics

Step through shock propagation at the integrator level.

The numerical shock engine, blast-wave evolution in power-law density profiles, and the time-stepping integrator from dynamics.

Shock Dynamics

Building Forward Models#

Assemble physical components into models that generate synthetic observables.

Physical Models

Generate synthetic light curves and SEDs with high-level model objects.

Chevalier shock models, FFA fadeout, TDE afterglows, Type IIn supernovae in dense CSM, and phenomenological light curves.

Forward Modeling
Accretion Disk Models

Evolve accretion disks through their thermodynamic phases.

Thermal S-curves, limit cycles, fallback disks, and advective solutions using the one_zone Cython integrator.

Accretion Disk Models

Running Inference#

Fit models to data and recover physical parameters from your observations.

Inference and Parameter Estimation

Fit a physical model to data and recover posterior distributions over its parameters.

End-to-end MCMC workflows — single- and multi-epoch SED fitting, upper-limit handling, shock-parameter recovery, and posterior propagation through closure relations. Built on the inference subpackage.

Inference and Parameter Estimation
