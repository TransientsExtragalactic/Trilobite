*****************************
Opacity Models
*****************************

These examples demonstrate the opacity classes available in Triceratops, covering both
analytic grey laws (Thomson electron scattering, Kramers free-free and bound-free) and
the table-based Rosseland mean opacity from the OPAL project
(Badnell et al. 2005, MNRAS 360:458).

.. rubric:: What you'll find here

- The canonical :math:`\kappa_R(T,\rho)` map from the bundled OPAL solar table —
  showing the electron-scattering plateau, Kramers power-law rise, iron-group opacity
  peak near :math:`T \sim 10^5` K, and low-temperature recombination drop.
- *(Planned)* Comparison of analytic (Kramers + ES) and OPAL table opacities across the
  accretion-disk parameter space.

:ref:`opacity_theory` — physical derivations and coordinate-system details
